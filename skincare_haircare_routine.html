Weeks 1–4 · Barrier reset, then introduce retinol

Goal: Reset your barrier. Same simple routine every day — no actives yet. AM is just water, no foam cleanser.
Goal: Introduce retinol slowly — Monday and Thursday only. Eye serum on 3 non-retinol nights. Wednesday and Friday are rest nights. If anything stings or flakes for more than 2 days, drop a night and double up on moisturizer.

As needed · Any week

🩹 Neutrogena Hydrocolloid Patches: stick on any whitehead overnight. Don't pop — let the patch do the work.

☀️ Sunscreen reapplication: reapply mid-day if outdoors. Especially important on retinol days — UV makes dark marks worse.

Weekly schedule + step-by-step task order

Goal: Calm intermittent flaking, restore moisture, and reduce breakage. Wash days are Monday, Wednesday, Saturday. Pre-wash oil treatment Sunday evening. Between-wash scalp serum Thursday. Always cool-rinse, scrunch dry with t-shirt or microfiber, detangle wet hair bottom-up with a wide-tooth comb.
How to use: Each card below is one type of session. Follow the steps top-to-bottom. The wash routine is your most-used one — Monday and Wednesday follow the standard version; Saturday adds the deep-condition variation.

Rules that override the schedule

🌡️ If scalp ever feels tight, itchy, or extra-flaky: skip oils for a week and just keep the Dove serum + Garnier wash. Reintroduce oils gradually.

💆 Massage technique: always fingertips, never nails. Small circles, 3–5 min for scalp. The massage matters as much as the product.

🛏️ Overnight oiling is optional: if Sunday's pre-wash oil sits more than ~1 hour, use an old pillowcase or wrap with a soft cotton tee. Don't go more than one night.

🌬️ Heat styling: always finish a blow dry on cool air to seal the cuticle — biggest single frizz reducer for wavy hair.

📅 Patience window: give the routine 4–6 weeks before judging shedding or breakage changes. Hair works on a slow cycle.
